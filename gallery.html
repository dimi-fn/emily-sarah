Freeflow

Freeflow for Emily was about expressing the sights of her home town in abstract formations

Motions...

Abstract for Emily has always been her favorite style, it gives her freedom to interpret things in her own way and show the world how she sees it through her eyes.

...Motions

The pieces take inspiration from Cy Twombly; one of her favourite artists in the mark making, which she has then paired alongside her pallet knife styling.

"Hard hitting colour, abstract and portrait art."

"Art is more than just a job; to me it's a therapy. Without it I truly wouldn't know how to Express myself"

"For me nature is my biggest inspiration. The textures around me and the intrigue behind how the paint can be manipulated to echo those sights. I love working on new looks and playing with new mediums and tools, be it a brush or a broom."

This series uses Golden acrylics alongside other mediums such as Spray paint, charcoal, pen and pastel. Pieces vary between paper and canvas originals.
